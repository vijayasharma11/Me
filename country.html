About My Birth Country: India

I was born in India, a country rich in history, culture, and diversity. Located in South Asia, India is the seventh-largest country by land area and the second-most populous country in the world, with over 1.4 billion people.

India is known for its diverse landscapes, from the snow-capped Himalayas in the north to the tropical beaches in the south. The country has a rich cultural heritage, with ancient traditions, festivals, and a diverse range of cuisines. Some of the most iconic landmarks include the Taj Mahal, Jaipur's Amber Fort, and the holy city of Varanasi along the banks of the Ganges River.

Here in India, you'll experience a blend of old-world charm and modern-day technology, with bustling cities like Mumbai and Delhi alongside serene villages and peaceful rural landscapes.
